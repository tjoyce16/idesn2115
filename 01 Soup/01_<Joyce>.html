Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Nature's Promise Chicken Broth
  • Chicken Tenders
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Your choice of pasta

Directions

  1. Chop up all of the veggies to your choosing. Include, Celery and Carrots. Other vegitables are opptional.
  2. Grab a pot and pour in two containers of the Natures Promise Chicken Broth.
  3. Fill the two empty containers with water, then pour the water into the pot.
  4. Add some salt and pepper to the soup along with some Italian Seasoning.
  5. Place the soup over medium heat.
  6. In a seperate pan, bring water to a boil.
  7. Slice the chicken breast down the middle, then dice up the thin strips.
  8. place chicken into the boiling water, and remove when white. When white, place into the broth to finish cooking with the soup.
  9. Place the cut up vegitables into the soup.
  10. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce to medium heat to finish cooking. Soup should be done in 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  11. Add your choice of pasta into the soup 15 minutes before done. one half cup to one cup should be enough.
  12. Enjoy your soup!
