2016 International Leadership Conference

Thursday, October 13th, 2016

How should organizations manage the uncertain and challenging world that confronts us here and abroad? Leaders have a special challenge in setting the direction of their organizations and understanding the implications of that direction for all their stakeholders, including the coming generation of leaders. Drawing on the experience of people who have led in the private sector, in public service, or at non-profit organizations, our annual conferences seek to identify the personal and organizational capacities that leaders will need and the steps that organizations can best take to build these capacities throughout the enterprise.
